C3S Mentor and Mentee(MAM)

Mission

To ensure the best international student experience at C3S Business School

Welcome to C3S Mentor and Mentee (MAM) website, where all the new and future C3S business school students can find their mentor who is a current student at the school. These mentors are there to guide you through your journey at C3S Business School and in Barcelona.




ABOUT ME AND C3S MAM

My name is Tatenda Getrude Mudzonga and I am currently a student at C3S Business School. My country of origin is Zimbabwe and I arrived in Spain, Barcelona in October 2023. The reason why I created this platform is because , I wanted future students of C3S Business School to find a current student to help them with their journey in Barcelona and C3S Business School. I know how hard it is to adapt to a new environment with different cultures and languages. So I wanted to help other students to find people who can advise and guide them with anything concerning school and social life in Barcelona. There are five mentors on this platform and hopefully we can increase the mentors as time goes on. I hope this platform will help you all. Good Luck !!!!
